My father went into debt to pay for my care, my mother sacrificed her job to stay by my side. My brothers did everything they could to put a smile back on my face and give me hope. Their love and support have been my lifeline in this storm. This story has no definitive ending. It is up to me to write the sequel, to fight for life and savor every precious moment.
